Research

Research questions that connect principled modeling with practical BCI performance.

My work sits at the intersection of neural signal processing, machine learning, and translational neurotechnology.

01 · BrainGate

Robust intracortical decoding across days

Investigating multi-gesture neural representations and decoder drift in human intracortical BCI, with an emphasis on stable performance under real longitudinal variability.

  • Cross-day decoder stabilization
  • Spike and multi-band LFP feature comparison
  • Generative alignment with flow matching and optimal transport

02 · THNE

Dynamics-aware neural decoding

Reframing continuous neural decoding as stable control inference under explicit dynamical priors, replacing unconstrained sequence regression with structured trajectory generation.

  • NeuroScribe 2.0 and state-space DMP decoding
  • Behavior-derived priors for cross-session robustness
  • Benchmarks on ECoG and multi-session spiking data

03 · Clinical BCI

Sleep-state detection for home-use BCI trials

Building automated neural pipelines to distinguish sleep and wake states from intracortical recordings, supporting safer and more reliable patient monitoring in clinical settings.

  • Low-frequency LFP analysis during spontaneous sleep
  • Sleep-versus-wake characterization in trial participants
  • Neural-state aware decoder performance monitoring
